About Bazquiat

The history of the brand

Born from art and tailoring

Bazquiat began as a small studio focused on blending gallery-inspired design with wearable silhouettes. The brand grew from custom wardrobe pieces into a full clothing house known for clean lines, premium fabrics, and confident styling.

Built for modern expression

Each collection celebrates individuality with a premium white-and-gold aesthetic. Today, Bazquiat creates capsule pieces that feel timeless, easy to combine, and elegant for everyday wear.
