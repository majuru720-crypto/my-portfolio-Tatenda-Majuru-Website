About Bazquiat

The history of the brand

Born from art and tailoring

Bazquiat began as a small design studio devoted to turning gallery-inspired ideas into wearable silhouettes. What started as custom wardrobe commissions evolved into a label recognized for its soft structure, premium materials, and confidence-led design language.

Built for modern expression

Today the brand creates capsule collections that feel timeless rather than trend-led. Every Bazquiat piece is designed to mix easily, photograph beautifully, and bring quiet luxury into everyday dressing.
